Services

We work hand-in-hand with you to provide an innovative solution to your particular problem.

You can either (1) submit the problem you are trying to solve and your solution idea, or (2) submit the problem alone and we'll come up with the solution.

Either step will be preceded by an initial concept development, a full 3D model, a first 3D printed prototype that will be shipped to you, and a final product based on your satisfaction with the prototype.

1. Idea definition

We clarify constraints, success criteria, and context so the build has a real target.

2. CAD design

Parametric, manufacturable CAD models designed for real-world use.

3. Prototyping

3D printing / fabrication for fit + function testing and fast iteration.

4. Small-batch builds

Once validated, we produce short runs or transition the design to a manufacturer.
