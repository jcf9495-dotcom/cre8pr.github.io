About CRE8 PR

CRE8 PR is an engineering design and prototyping studio focused on turning early-stage ideas into real, functional objects.

The studio was created to bridge the gap between concepts and manufacturable solutions — combining CAD, rapid prototyping, and hands-on iteration to validate ideas early and efficiently.

What we do

We help individuals, startups, and small teams take ideas from rough sketches to physical prototypes. Our work emphasizes engineering rigor, manufacturability, and real-world constraints.

How we work

Projects typically move through concept development, CAD design, prototyping, and refinement. Each step is driven by testing and feedback, not assumptions.

Why CRE8 PR

CRE8 PR was built with a focus on practical engineering and accessibility. By operating from Puerto Rico, we aim to support local innovation while working with clients anywhere.

Whether you have a fully formed idea or just a problem to solve, the goal is the same: build something real, test it early, and improve it fast.

The studio’s work includes mechanical prototypes, CAD-driven design studies, and iterative builds developed through hands-on testing and refinement. Each project emphasizes manufacturability and real-world constraints rather than purely conceptual designs.
